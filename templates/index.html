WELCOME TO AVIAN CALLS

Welcome to Avian Calls, a sophisticated bird sound classifier utilizing an advanced SVM model. Our objective is to provide a seamless and intuitive way for bird enthusiasts and researchers to identify bird species through their calls. By simply uploading an audio recording, our machine learning model accurately predicts the bird species, presenting you with essential details such as the bird's name, scientific classification, and habitat. Additionally, we offer visual aids including images, geographical map locations, and audio samples to enhance your learning experience. Avian Calls serves as a valuable tool for ornithologists, bird watchers, and nature lovers, fostering a deeper understanding and appreciation of avian diversity.
